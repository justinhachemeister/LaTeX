\huge{Let us define
$$\mathcal{I}=\begin{cases}
\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}-\alpha,\dfrac{\pi}2\right)\cup
\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}-\alpha,\dfrac{3\pi}2\right),\\ 
\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad\text{if }0<\alpha<\dfrac{\pi}{2}\\
\left[0,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)\cup
\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}-\alpha,\dfrac{3\pi}2\right)\\ 
\qquad\cup\left(\dfrac{5\pi}{2}-\alpha,2\pi\right],\text{if }\dfrac{\pi}{2}<\alpha<\pi 
\end{cases}$$

Then for $\theta\in [0,2\pi]$ we have
$$f(\theta)=1\iff \theta\in\mathcal{I}$$}
